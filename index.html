About

Hi, I'm Jack Baron, a teenage boy living in England. I run a few YouTube Channels, detailed HERE. I like all things computer related, and I am currently doing my GCSEs.

I am currently a Moderator on the Offical NerdCubed Servers. Come hang out on Just Cause 2 Multiplayer or TeamSpeak

I am also the proud owner of an Oculus Rift DK2, which I'm quite happy about.

I love making things (small or large), and as such I know HTML, VB, JavaScript, a bit of Python, and I am learning Java. At some point I may upload all my projects here.

On the side, I write android apps. You can find them all HERE

YouTube
